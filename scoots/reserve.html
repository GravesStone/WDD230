Rental Services and Agreements

  • Reservation Confirmation: A $50 security deposit is required to confirm reservations.
  • Driver Requirements: A valid driver's license is required at the time of rental.
  • Age Restriction: No one under the age of 12 years is allowed to ride on the scooters.
  • Fuel Charge: Fuel charge is not included in the rental fee.
  • Mileage Policy: Unlimited mileage is provided.
  • Insurance Coverage: Liability insurance is included in the rental package. Damage insurance is available for purchase.
  • Delivery Service: Free delivery to Cozumel hotels or Airbnbs for rentals of 2 days or more.
  • Payment Options: Payment accepted via Google Pay, Apple Pay, cash, credit card, debit card, or Venmo.
  • Return Policy: Rentals may be returned to either physical location.
