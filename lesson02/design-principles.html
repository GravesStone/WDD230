white space and clean design

pathway connect

www.byupathway.org

white spaces are an important part of a clear and clean design in web development. the human eye can understand better the content and gives a sense of simplicity and sophisticated lucidity.

it's considered best practices the following:

  • max 50 - 60 characters per line
  • 2 - 3 fonts max
  • bold the important idea
  • clear images and spacing.
  • high-quality images
  • correct use of padding and borders
  • aligned grids and no extra spaces

contrast

lush

https://lush.com.ph/

it's important in contrast to be clear with the difference between the foreground and background.

here there are best practices good to apply:

  • visual hierarchy
  • font sizes
  • image and content placement
  • contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1
  • large-scale text and images: 3:1;
  • text or images part of an inactive user interface has no contrast
  • in case of logotypes: no contrast

repetition

microsoft

https://www.microsoft.com/en-ph

the principle of repetition teaches us how important is to be consistent in our structure,layout,and schemes.

some of the best practices of repetition are:

  • consistency in the use of colors
  • font sizes
  • abundant use of logo and brand
  • focus on strengthening identity in styling
  • patterns: design elements working in unison
  • consistent white spacing
  • effective use of a pattern of contrasts
